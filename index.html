The Maha Darwaja (Great Gate) is the grand main entrance of Raigad Fort, built under the rule of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj in the 17th century. It was designed as a symbol of Maratha power, with massive stone walls and strong wooden doors.

The gate was built in such a way that war elephants or enemy forces could not easily break in.
